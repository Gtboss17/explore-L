    About Explore London

    Our Vision

    Explore London helps travelers and locals discover the magic of London — from its iconic landmarks to hidden gems.

    Mission

    We aim to make trip planning fun, visual, and effortless. Whether you’re exploring with friends, family, or solo — our tools empower your experience.

    Key Features

    • Category-based attraction browsing
    • Filter by price, distance, or popularity
    • Customizable trip itinerary builder
    • Live interactive map with markers and details

    Tech Stack

    • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
    • Bootstrap 5 for layout and responsiveness
    • Leaflet.js for real-time maps
    • LocalStorage to save user data
